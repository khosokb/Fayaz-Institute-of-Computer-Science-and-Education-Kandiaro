Eligibility Criteria & Requirements

Minimum Academic Requirements

  • Intermediate / 10+2 (Pre-Engineering / General Science)
  • Equivalency certificate required for foreign degrees.
  • Minimum aggregate of 50% in previous qualification.

Documents Required

  • Recent Passport Size Photographs (3)
  • Attested Copies of Educational Certificates
  • Copy of CNIC / B-Form
  • Affidavit (on stamp paper) for rules compliance.
